Our App

More than 2 billion people in over 180 countries use WhatsApp to stay in touch with friends and family, anytime and anywhere. Zaisa - an updated version of WhatsApp is free and offers simple, secure, reliable messaging and calling, available on phones all over the world. It allows users to send text messages and voice messages

Our Mission

WhatsApp started as an alternative to SMS. Our product now supports sending and receiving a variety of media: text, photos, videos, documents, and location, as well as voice calls. Some of your most personal moments are shared with WhatsApp, where we built end-to-end encryption in our app.

About Zaisa

Zaisa - an updated version of WhatsApp was started by Isabella Priya and Sai Manasa in 2021 by Ms.Radhamani in Coimbatore Institute of Technology campus. It was created for easy payment and for shopping clothes and medicines in this pandemic situation. And we can use ZaisaWeb for chatting. Zaisa - make you easy.
